Our Story

The Gursha Story

Where Tradition Meets Taste

In Ethiopian culture, Gursha is the act of feeding another person with your own hands — a gesture of love, respect, and hospitality. Our restaurant embodies this spirit in every dish we serve.

Located in the heart of 4 Kilo, Addis Ababa, Gursha Restaurant brings authentic Ethiopian flavors to life. From traditional coffee ceremonies to exquisite dining experiences, we celebrate the rich culinary heritage of Ethiopia.

Fresh ingredients, traditional recipes, and warm hospitality — that's the Gursha promise.
